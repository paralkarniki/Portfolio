Coffee Maker Simulator

Coffee Maker Simulator

Technologies : Swift, IOS, Java, JSP, MYSQL, Spring MVC framework, JavaScript, Ajax, jQuery, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS5


• Designed and developed in Swift IOS development
• Project concentrated on the aspect of the utilizing the ios simulator to make a user friendly coffee machine that give the users a chance pick and make the drink as they desire.
